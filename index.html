Problem Statement

Over 70% of rural Nigeria lacks electricity, severely impacting education, healthcare, and economic growth.

Analysis Findings

Metric Value
Average Electricity Access (2000-2020) ~50%
Renewable Energy Share ~10-15%
Predicted 2030 Access ~70%

Data analyzed using Python, with linear regression for future predictions.

Sustainable Solution

Proposing solar energy adoption in low-access regions for a sustainable future, leveraging renewable potential.

Region Proposed Action
Rural North Solar Mini-Grids
South-East Solar Home Systems
